German Pancake Recipe

German pancakes, also known as Dutch babies, are a delicious and fluffy breakfast treat that puffs up in the oven. They are easy to make and can be topped with various fruits, syrups, or powdered sugar.


Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk (240ml)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (120g)
  • dash of salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

  2. As oven preheats, put the butter in an un-greased cast iron pan and place in oven, just until melted.

  3. Place the eggs, milk, flour, salt and vanilla in a blender; cover and process until smooth. Pour batter into baking dish, over melted butter.

  4. Bake, for 22-27 minutes or until edges are golden brown and puffy.

  5. To serve, sprinkle generously with powdered sugar and syrup or a mix of fresh fruits or berries.
